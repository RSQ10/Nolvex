Common Questions
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees on any plan. The monthly retainer covers everything — strategy, setup, creatives, management, and reporting. What you see is what you pay.
What's the minimum commitment?
All plans require a minimum 3-month engagement. Paid advertising takes time to optimize — typically 30–45 days to reach peak performance. The 3-month window ensures we can deliver real results rather than just initial data.
Is my ad spend included in the plan cost?
No — the plan fee covers management, strategy, and creative. Your ad spend budget is separate and paid directly to the ad platforms (Facebook/Google/LinkedIn). We manage it on your behalf within the limits outlined in each plan.
Can I upgrade my plan mid-engagement?
Absolutely. Many clients start on Basic or Sweet and upgrade as results build. Upgrades can happen at the start of any new billing month. There's no penalty or additional fee for switching tiers.
Do you work with businesses outside the USA?
Yes — we now work with India-based businesses as well. Discovery calls are available at times convenient for both EST and IST timezones. All plans and pricing remain the same regardless of your location.
